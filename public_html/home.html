Project Description

What is MCM?

The goal of MCM is to curate and organize predicted bacterial contributions
to metabolites in transgenic claudin phenotypes of Mus musculus.

~100 metabolic pathways are available for query, showcasing the contributions of
differentially abundant bacteria to each pathway.
